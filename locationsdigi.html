Locations:

In New Zealand, there are not many options for HADO.

Shore Junction
Shore Junction is a free-to-access youth innovation hub in Takapuna, Auckland, designed for young people aged 13-24 to connect, belong, and grow, offering facilities and services that support innovation, learning, well-being, and creativity. Shore Junction also has an area set up for HADO.

Rangitoto College
For some of the HADO cups and some of the HADO practices, during the weekend on specific days, you can play HADO in the Rangitoto Center.

School Visits
HADO NZ also offers school visits, so students can learn about Augmented Reality Technology (AR), and have the opportunity to play HADO in their own school.
